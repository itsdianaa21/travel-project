Why New York?

New York, the City of Dreams

With so many movies and shows showcasing New York City, it is rare to find someone who does not want to know what New York actually feels like. It is a modern city, with residents from all around the world. New York is a multicultural city with every neighborhood having its own unique characteristics adding its own textures to this city.

New York, AKA the Big Apple, has a breathtaking skyline that keeps everyone in awe. NY Times Square, is a popular tourist place. The city boasts its passion for art and architecture. Also, witness some of the most iconic buildings in the city on Wall Street. New York is never-ending, with every turn a traveller takes, there is something new to explore.
